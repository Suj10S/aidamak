Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

How does the AI-powered matching system work?

Our AI system analyzes your academic background, interests, career goals, and preferences to match you with the most suitable universities and programs. It considers factors like admission requirements, scholarship opportunities, location preferences, and career outcomes to provide personalized recommendations.

What is the success rate of your visa applications?

We maintain a 95% success rate for student visa applications. Our experts provide comprehensive guidance on documentation, interview preparation, and follow-up procedures to maximize your chances of approval.

How long does the university application process take?

The complete process typically takes 3-6 months, depending on the country and university requirements. This includes profile evaluation, document preparation, test preparation (if needed), application submission, and visa processing. We recommend starting at least 9-12 months before your intended intake.

Do you guarantee admission to universities?

While we cannot guarantee admission as final decisions are made by universities, we have a 95% success rate in securing admissions for our students. We optimize your application to maximize your chances and typically apply to 5-8 universities to ensure multiple options.

What makes your consultancy different from others?

Our unique combination of AI technology and human expertise sets us apart. We provide data-driven recommendations while maintaining personalized support from experienced counselors. Our end-to-end services cover everything from university selection to pre-departure orientation, and we have partnerships with 50+ universities worldwide.
